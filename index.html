A programmer is someone who uses coding languages like Python, Java, or JavaScript to build software, websites, and apps. They break down complex problems into smaller tasks, writing code that computers can understand. Their work involves not only creating new systems but also debugging and improving existing ones, ensuring that everything runs smoothly. Programmers collaborate with other developers, designers, and clients to turn ideas into functional, efficient programs. They need strong problem-solving skills, creativity, and the ability to adapt to new technologies and tools.
